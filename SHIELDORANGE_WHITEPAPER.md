**The Gap:** Those who predict weather damage 48-72 hours early gain a systematic trading edge.

**Operational Security:**
- Multi-sig wallet (founders + 3 community members)
- Hot wallet <5% of capital (cold storage for remainder)
- Monthly security audits
- Disaster recovery procedures documented

**Weather Prediction Accuracy:**
- **Risk:** AI predictions may be wrong (30% historical error rate)
- **Mitigation:** Conservative position sizing, mandatory stop-losses, diversification across event types
- **Impact:** Single wrong prediction = max -15% loss on 30% of capital = -4.5% total capital drawdown

**Market Volatility:**
- **Risk:** OJ futures markets can be highly volatile and illiquid
- **Mitigation:** VIX-adjusted position sizing, correlation limits, maintain 20% cash reserve
- **Impact:** Reduced position sizes during high volatility protect capital

**Black Swan Events:**
- **Risk:** Unprecedented weather events outside historical database
- **Mitigation:** Maximum position sizing caps, diversification across geographies (future expansion)
- **Impact:** No single event can destroy entire fund (max 30% per trade)

### Operational Risks

**Technology Failure:**
- **Risk:** NOAA API downtime, satellite data unavailable, AI model errors
- **Mitigation:** Multiple data source redundancy, manual override capabilities, comprehensive testing
- **Impact:** Missed predictions (not capital loss), opportunity cost only

**Security Breaches:**
- **Risk:** Wallet compromise, smart contract vulnerabilities
- **Mitigation:** Multi-sig wallet (3-of-5), cold storage (95% of funds), regular security audits
- **Impact:** Funds protected by multiple signature requirements

**Regulatory Changes:**
- **Risk:** Commodity trading regulations, token classification changes
- **Mitigation:** Legal counsel, compliance monitoring, jurisdictional flexibility
- **Impact:** Potential need to restructure operations

### Market Risks

**Token Price Volatility:**

- **Impact:** Long-term holders benefit from distributions regardless of price volatility

**Liquidity Challenges:**
- **Risk:** Low trading volume could make buybacks difficult

### Performance Risks

**Below-Target Returns:**
